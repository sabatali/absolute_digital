    The Client

    Ladbrokes is the UK’s biggest bookmarker and the world’s most trusted gambling site, offering
    sports betting, horse racing and financial betting.

    The Solution

    Full, in-depth site analysis to determine the effectiveness of previous SEO strategies, and to extract additional information to determine strategy.

    Develop a number of creative campaigns, integrating games and infographics.

    Work closely with the client to conceptualise brand voice, establish tone & branding, while also meeting the industry’s stringent compliance standards.

    Optimise existing revenue-generating pages and identify keyword & creative gaps in the News section.

    The Challenge

    Having seen our previous success in online casino outreach campaigns, Ladbrokes was keen to work with us to implement a highly creative, targeted outreach campaign that would provide sustainable ranking increases for some extremely competitive keyword phrases. A major focus of the campaign was on content marketing, in order to promote the brand as an industry leader while providing valuable information in a fun and creative way on their site.
